# Privacy Policy for DocuMail Pro

Your privacy is a top priority. DocuMail Pro handles data strictly in compliance with Google API User Data Policies.

1. **Data Access & Scope:** The add-on requests access to scopes like Google Sheets, Google Documents, Google Drive, and Gmail solely to execute your configured automation tasks (e.g., pulling row data, generating PDFs, and sending emails on your behalf).
2. **Data Storage:** DocuMail Pro operates entirely client-side or within your isolated Google Apps Script environment. We do not transmit, collect, or store your spreadsheet data, email text, or personal information on external third-party servers.
3. **Third-Party Sharing:** No user data is ever shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes. All data operations remain localized to your active Google Workspace ecosystem.
